BSc Computer Science

BSc Computer Science is a 3-year undergraduate degree that focuses on the fundamentals of computer science, including programming, algorithms, data structures, and operating systems..

VISION & MISSION OF BSc Computer Science


VISION

To prepare students for a variety of careers in the rapidly growing field of computer science.


MISSION

To provide students with the knowledge and skills necessary to become successful computer scientists, including a strong foundation in the theoretical foundations of computer science, the ability to apply computer science concepts to real-world problems, and the ability to think critically and solve problems creatively.

Job opportunities

  • Software Developer: Software developers are responsible for designing, developing, and testing software applications. They work in a variety of industries, including technology, healthcare, and finance.
  • Web Developer: Web developers are responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining websites. They work in a variety of industries, including e-commerce, media, and education.
  • Data Scientist: Data scientists are responsible for collecting, cleaning, and analyzing data. They work in a variety of industries, including technology, healthcare, and retail.
  • Information Security Analyst: Information security analysts are responsible for protecting computer systems and networks from unauthorized access. They work in a variety of industries, including government, finance, and healthcare.
  • Network Engineer: Network engineers are responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining computer networks. They work in a variety of industries, including technology, healthcare, and finance.
