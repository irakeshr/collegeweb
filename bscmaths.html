BSc Mathematics

BSc Mathematics is a 3-year undergraduate degree that focuses on developing mathematical skills of the candidates along with the analysis of numbers.

VISION & MISSION OF BSc Mathematics


VISION

To provide students with a rigorous and comprehensive education in mathematics that will prepare them for a variety of careers, including research, teaching, and business.


MISSION

To impart the knowledge and skills necessary for students to become competent users of mathematics, to understand the use of mathematics in other disciplines, and to develop a love of learning.

Job opportunities

  • Actuarial Science: Actuaries use mathematics and statistics to assess risk and uncertainty. They work in a variety of industries, including insurance, finance, and healthcare
  • Teaching: Mathematicians are in high demand as teachers at all levels, from elementary school to college.
  • Data Science: Data scientists use mathematics and statistics to analyze large datasets. They work in a variety of industries, including technology, healthcare, and retail.
  • Research: Mathematicians can pursue research careers in academia, government, or industry.
  • Finance: Mathematicians are highly valued in the financial industry, where they work as analysts, traders, and investment bankers.
