BA English

BA English is a 3-year undergraduate degree that focuses on the study of English language and literature.

VISION & MISSION OF BA English


VISION

To foster an appreciation for the richness and diversity of the English language and literature.


MISSION

To provide students with the knowledge and skills they need to become effective communicators, critical thinkers, and creative writers.

Job opportunities

  • Teaching: English majors are well-suited for teaching positions at the elementary, middle, and high school levels. They may also be qualified to teach English as a second language (ESL) or as a foreign language.
  • Writing: English majors can find work as writers in a variety of fields, including journalism, marketing, public relations, and technical writing. They may also work as freelance writers or bloggers.
  • Editing: English majors have the skills necessary to become editors in a variety of fields, including publishing, journalism, and marketing. They may work as copy editors, proofreaders, or developmental editors.
  • Publishing: English majors can find work in the publishing industry as editors, marketers, or salespeople. They may also work as book reviewers or literary agents.
