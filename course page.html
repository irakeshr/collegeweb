Our Courses

Courses We Offer

Our wide range of courses will help you achieve your academic goals.

BSc Mathematics

BSc Mathematics is a 3-year undergraduate degree that focuses on developing mathematical skills of the candidates along with the analysis of numbers.

BSc Physics

BSc Physics is a 3-year undergraduate degree that focuses on the study of matter, energy, and their interactions..

BSc Computer Science

BSc Computer Science is a 3-year undergraduate degree that focuses on the fundamentals of computer science, including programming, algorithms, data structures, and operating systems..

BA English

BA English is a 3-year undergraduate degree that focuses on the study of English language and literature.

B.Com Finance

BCom Finance is a 3-year undergraduate degree that focuses on the study of financial markets, institutions, and instruments.

B.Com CA

BCom CA is a 3-year undergraduate degree that prepares students for a career in accounting and finance.

BBA

BBA is a 3-year undergraduate degree that focuses on the study of business administration.

BSc Statistics

BSc Statistics is a 3-year undergraduate degree that focuses on the study of statistics, which is the science of collecting, organizing, analyzing, and interpreting data.

MSc Computer Science

MSc Computer Science is a 2-year postgraduate degree that focuses on the advanced study of computer science.

Our Facilities

college library

College ground

We are proud of our football ground and we are committed to keeping it in good condition. We believe that it is an important part of our campus and we are grateful to the people who use it.

Fitness Center

Our college's fitness center is well-equipped with new and state-of-the-art equipment, making it a great place to stay fit and healthy

NCC

Our college offers the National Cadet Corps (NCC) as an extracurricular activity, providing students with the opportunity to learn valuable leadership and teamwork skills, as well as experience the discipline and camaraderie of military life.

NSS

Our college is proud to offer the National Service Scheme (NSS) to its students. This program provides students with the opportunity to give back to their community and to learn valuable life skills.
