BSc Physics

BSc Physics is a 3-year undergraduate degree that focuses on the study of matter, energy, and their interactions..

VISION & MISSION OF BSc Physics


VISION

To provide students with a rigorous and comprehensive education in physics that will prepare them for a variety of careers, including research, teaching, and engineering.


MISSION

To impart the knowledge and skills necessary for students to become competent users of physics, to understand the use of physics in other disciplines, and to develop a love of learning.

Job opportunities

  • Physics Teacher: Physics teachers are in high demand at all levels, from elementary school to college. They use their knowledge of physics to explain complex concepts to students and help them develop critical thinking skills.
  • Research Scientist: Research scientists use their knowledge of physics to conduct research in a variety of fields, such as medicine, engineering, and materials science. They work in universities, government agencies, and private industry.
  • Engineer: Engineers use their knowledge of physics to design and build products and systems. They work in a variety of industries, such as aerospace, automotive, and construction.
  • Technician: Technicians use their knowledge of physics to operate and maintain scientific equipment. They work in laboratories, hospitals, and other settings.
  • Data Scientist: Data scientists use their knowledge of physics to analyze large datasets. They work in a variety of industries, such as technology, healthcare, and retail.
